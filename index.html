Biography

I am currently a senior student majoring in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University. I am an undergraduate researcher working at Optical Imaging Systems Lab, advised by Prof. Seung Ah Lee. I am passionate about computational imaging, lensless imaging, laser speckle imaging, ptychography, and other innovative ways of using imaging systems to expand the limits of vision. Previously, I worked at Smart Imaging Lab, advised by Prof. Guoan Zheng.

Interests

  • Depth of Field Extension
  • Optimization
  • Spatially-coded Fourier Ptychography
  • Speckle Plethysmography
  • Inverse Problems
  • End-to-end Imaging System
